ABOUT US

Welcome to our House Price Prediction web application! This tool is designed to help homeowners,buyers and real estate professionals estimate property prices based on multiple factors such as square footage, number of bedrooms and bathrooms, location coordinates and various property features. The app uses advanced machine learning models like Linear Regression and Random Forest, trained on a robust dataset to provide accurate predictions.
